Power On - App Design

UI/UX

Power On is a online platform designed to help a community solve a hypothetical outage issue in downtown Manhattan, where each building has intermittent water and electricity outages, both scheduled and unscheduled.

The outages are the main focus of the app, so the daily and weekly display of them are right on the homepage, along with any unscheduled outages that others have reported. The report function is also on the homepage, so users can easily submit their own reports. Additionally, a sorting feature allows users to easily find the outages that are of most concern to them.

While designing the forum, I initially wanted to include a variety of features, like private messages. However I figured that the point of the platform was about communicating these outages, not anything else, so I held back on those features. Instead, I included a phone number and email option in the profile, so users could find a contact point if they needed to.
